Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2026

Introduction

Labour Lekka ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection

Labour Lekka is designed to work completely offline. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you enter into the app remains stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All information you input into Labour Lekka, including:

  • Labour records and attendance data
  • Employee information
  • Work schedules and timesheets
  • Any other data entered by you

is stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Labour Lekka does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking technologies, or any form of analytics to monitor your usage of the app.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a secure lock screen on your device
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Regularly backing up your device data

Children's Privacy

Labour Lekka does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is intended for business and professional use.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support channels.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Modify or delete any information at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data from your device

Consent

By using Labour Lekka, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
