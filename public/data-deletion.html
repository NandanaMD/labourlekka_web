Data Deletion Guide

Labour Lekka

You have full control of your data. Labour Lekka is a privacy-first, offline app. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, access, or share any of your information with third parties or for advertising purposes.

How Your Data is Stored

Labour Lekka stores all your worker information, attendance records, and payment data locally on your device. The app works completely offline and does not require an account.

The only optional feature that connects online is Google Drive Backup, which stores a copy of your data in your own Google Drive — not on our servers. This is entirely optional and controlled by you.

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your data at any time using any of the following methods:

Method 1: Sign Out from Google Drive (Cloud Backup)

If you've linked your Google Drive for backup, you can disconnect it anytime:

  1. Open the Labour Lekka app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Cloud Backup
  4. Tap Sign Out

Note: This only disconnects the Google Drive sync. Your local data on the device remains intact. To delete local data, use Method 2.

Method 2: Reset App (Delete All Local Data)

To permanently delete all worker records, attendance, and payment data from your device:

  1. Open the Labour Lekka app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to DANGER ZONE
  4. Tap Reset App
  5. Confirm the action

⚠️ Warning: This will permanently erase all data from your device. Make sure you have a backup if you need to keep any records.

Method 3: Delete Backup Files from Google Drive

If you've enabled Google Drive backup, you can manually delete the backed-up files:

  1. Open Google Drive (via web or mobile app)
  2. Navigate to the folder where Labour Lekka stores backups
  3. Select the backup files
  4. Delete them permanently

Note: Since the data is stored in your Google Drive, you have complete access and control. We cannot access or delete these files — only you can.

Method 4: Unlink App from Google Account

To revoke Labour Lekka's access to your Google account:

  1. Go to your Google Account Permissions
  2. Find Labour Lekka in the list
  3. Click Remove Access

Note: This prevents the app from syncing with your Google Drive in the future, but does not delete existing files in your Drive. You'll need to manually delete those (see Method 3).

Method 5: Uninstall the App

Simply uninstalling Labour Lekka from your device will remove all local data:

  1. Long-press the Labour Lekka app icon
  2. Select Uninstall
  3. Confirm

Note: If you had Google Drive backup enabled, the backup files will remain in your Google Drive. You can delete them manually using Method 3 if desired.

Complete Data Deletion Checklist

To ensure all your data is completely removed:

  1. Sign out from Google Drive (if connected)
  2. Reset the app from Settings → DANGER ZONE
  3. Delete backup files from your Google Drive (if any)
  4. Unlink Labour Lekka from your Google Account permissions
  5. Uninstall the app from your device
